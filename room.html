Archiving

Collect any object personally meaningful to you; document their names, appearances, odors, and the ways they trigger narratives about yourself.

Ongoing by Scene Peng ↪



B

(aggressive)
Bootlegging

Embrace the post-modern reality; copy it, appropriate it and steal from it, until you truly owns it.

Bootlegging Clothing by Libby Marrs ↪



C

(cathartic)
Cooking

Write your personal recipe and self-caring dishes; document the ways they make you feel when you have them.

Chinese Protest Recipes by Ronald Tau ↪



D

(nostalgic)
Dériving

Discard the map and go to uncharted places; follow your instincts and wander in the city; go find out the sunniest spot and have a picnic with your friends there.

The Five Diversions by Qihang Li ↪



F

(enlightened)
Fortune-telling

Keep a deck of tarot cards with you; let your instincts and inspirations guide you to make decisions.

Shaman Trip by Wenkai Wang ↪



G

(hybrid)
Growing

Grow up a life, water it and feed it; see how it inherits or mutates; document its inter-dependency with you.

Future Footwear by Sara Park ↪



J

(intensive)
Journaling

Document your stream of consciousness; create a personal repository for it.

Postit by Joss Liao ↪



M

(romantic)
Mailing

Write letters in your mother tongue and send them to your friends; encipher and decipher letters with your own coding systems.

Digi Cards by Kevin Dong and Raina Wellman ↪



N

(meditative)
Nailpolishing

Polish every part of your body carefully.

Nailpolishing by Misuzu Shibano ↪



P

(urgent)
Performing

Exaggerate your physical body languages; use your movements to speak; feel your body, listen to it and let it empowers you.

Emergency Suit! by Niya Sun and Libby Marrs ↪



R

(flamboyant)
Rapping

Turn slangs into spoken-word poems; sing them aloud on the street to praise or critique.

Rap by Yiqun Zhou ↪



S

(chimeric)
Styling

Imagine how the habitants on your planet would look like; wear like them and document the ways in which clothing empowers you.

Killer Rabit by Yu Dong ↪



W

(enchanting)
Wording

Map out a list of your favorite words; see how they relate to each other and how they develop their own senses of poetics.

Reading Machines by Tiger Dingsun ↪



Z

(blissful)
Zooming

Unite with friends and gather around with the people you love; support others, and get through the (hard) time together.

Class 2020 Graduation by RISD GD Department ↪

This project is composed of three layers of narrative: ① an archive of people’s previous projection into the future of 2020; ② an essay on worldbuilding as a daily practice; ③ a meditation room of people’s daily stories in the face of a global crisis.

© 2020 Ningjing Sun; Last updated in November 2020
Special thanks to Marie Otsuka and Minkyoung Kim

“Post-”, as the prefix meaning “after”, also points to how people are dealing with their lives as the year of 2020 unfolds dramatically in the face of a global crisis. By collecting people’s personal daily practices as forms of creative resistance, the last part of this project—the meditation room A–Z aims to recognize and praise our daily efforts in surviving these crazy times, since true heroism resides in those seemingly small ways of organising one’s life and seeking comfort, security and certainty from our daily routines in order to live a life without losing dignity.
