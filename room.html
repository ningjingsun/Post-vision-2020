CHAPTER 3

“Post-”, as the prefix meaning “after”, points to how people are dealing with their lives as the year 2020 unfolds dramatically. By collecting people’s personal daily practices as forms of creative resistance, the third chapter of this project aims to recognize our daily efforts in surviving the crazy times, since true heroism resides in those seemingly small ways of organising one’s life and seeking comfort and security from our daily routines in order to live a life without losing hope.
