2020

Written and designed by Ningjing Sun

Last updated: December 2020

2020 is a point of transformation. As we are about to enter the post-2020 era, this is a place for you to find new connections, retain your intimacy, and protect your own vulnerability.



This project is composed of four layers: ① an archive of people’s previous projections into the future of 2020; ② an essay on worldbuilding as a daily practice; ③ a meditation room of people’s daily stories in the face of a global crisis; ④ a garden of gifts and offerings that will prepare you for your future advantures.
