This project is composed of three layers of narrative: ① an archive of people’s previous projection into the future of 2020; ② a collection of people’s daily stories in the face of a global crisis; ③ a short essay on worldbuilding as a daily practice.

Special thanks to Marie Otsuka and Minkyoung Kim

2020 is a big year and the beginning of a new decade. This calendar below acts as an archive of people’s previous predictions about 2020. It aims to excavate and collect these predictions, projections, lost hopes and wishes that people made about 2020 before its actual arrival—some positive, some negative, but most of them unfulfilled due to the global pandemic—and weave them together into a parallel, fictitious narrative of the year.
